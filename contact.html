Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about stock, shipping, and certification.

Do you provide Mill Test Certificates (MTC)?

Yes, absolutely. Every dispatch is accompanied by a Mill Test Certificate (MTC) in accordance with EN 10204 3.1 standards, certifying chemical composition and mechanical properties. We also provide heat number traceability from the manufacturing mill.

What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

For stock items (standard sheets, coils, pipes), the MOQ is 500 kg. For custom sizes or special grades not in stock, the MOQ may vary between 1-3 tons depending on the mill's rolling requirements. Contact us for specific grade MOQs.

Do you export materials internationally?

Yes! We export to over 15 countries including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kenya, Nigeria, and Southeast Asia. We handle all logistics including sea-worthy wooden pallet packing, container stuffing, and complete documentation (Commercial Invoice, Packing List, COA, Fumigation Certificate, Certificate of Origin).

What payment terms do you accept?

For domestic clients: We accept RTGS/NEFT, cheques, and for established clients, credit terms of 30-60 days. For exports: We work with LC (Letter of Credit), TT (Telegraphic Transfer), and for regular buyers, credit arrangements.

Can you cut material to custom sizes?

Yes. We have plasma/laser cutting facilities for sheets and plates. We can also arrange pipe cutting and beveling. Custom cutting is usually completed within 2-3 days. Cutting charges apply based on complexity and quantity.

How quickly can you deliver within India?

Within Mumbai: Same-day/next-day delivery for stock items.
Maharashtra: 2-3 days.
Pan-India: 4-7 days via road transport. We work with trusted logistics partners for safe material handling.
