Our History

From Local Trader to Global Stockist

Our journey began in 1992 with the establishment of Nagmani Metal Corporation. What started as a vision to supply quality metal to local industries in Mumbai has today evolved into a global powerhouse.

Under the seasoned guidance of Mr. Champalal Jain, the company built a reputation for unshakeable integrity. His philosophy was simple: "Sell quality, or don't sell at all."

Today, rebranded as Tatvam Overseas and led by the dynamic 3rd Generation, we combine this traditional wisdom with modern logistics, serving clients across the Middle East, Europe, and Africa.

Quality Assurance

Approved by the Best.
